GSD - German Sheperd

Out of 100 popular dog breeds, studies have shown that German
Shepherds are the 3rd most intelligent.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Size: 24-26in Male, 22-24in Female
  • Life Span: 9 – 13 years
  • Fun Fact: A German Shepherd’s bite has 238 pounds of force

Known for its loyal, protective, and versatile nature, the
German Shepherd is a strong, athletic, and inteligent breed.
